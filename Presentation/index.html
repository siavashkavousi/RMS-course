Computational Environment

After construction phase is Execution phase!

Executes graphs in the context of Sessions


                    sess = tf.Session()
                    result = sess.run(product)
                    print(result)
                    # Close the Session when we're done.
                    sess.close()

or


                    with tf.Session() as sess:
                        result = sess.run([product])
                        print(result)
